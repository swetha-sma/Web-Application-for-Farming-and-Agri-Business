BRIEF DESCRIPTION:

  “For of all gainful professions, nothing is better, nothing more pleasing, nothing more delightful, nothing better becomes a well-bred man than agriculture.” Agriculture plays an important role in India. With an immense amount land, rich soils, and multiple climates agriculture is one of the most important economies in India. Farming is a profession with hard labor and effort. Moreover seeing the growing population of our country we should take initiatives to help farmers of our country. Our project work is designed to find out appraisal of problems and prospect of peasant farming in our country. During the study, data was collected through the use of questionnaire and survey. In this survey taken we came to know the importance of communication needs to be viewed as a core value in farming business. Effective communication is something that should be expected, routine and rewarded. The study therefore focused on developing a website to help farmers ensure greater profitability through direct farmer to supplier and farmer to farmer communication. So we decided to bring a solution named ‘AG Beyond’. As agricultural producers strive to improve production methods and productivity, they shouldn’t forget that communication with others can help get the job done. Information and Communication service are not always easy to access in rural areas. So¸ therefore by creating a web page it helps the farmers to communicate throughout the Tamil Nadu and in between the districts. The productivity will also increase. And the farmers can get to know the availability of crops varieties around their district which will help other farmers to cultivate new crop varieties in their area. The website serves as a business card, a salesperson, a shop and a plant-savvy best friend. This helps to make shopping more social where for the non-agriculture people to get the crops directly from the farmers. People will be given an opportunity to know where your food comes from when they communicate with the farmers. They can also learn some great tips and recipes from farmers and fellow patrons. These ideas automatically pave a way to support the local economy.
